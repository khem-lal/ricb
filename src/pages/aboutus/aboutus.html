About Us About Us The Royal Insurance Corporation of Bhutan Limited (RICB) is the country's second oldest financial institute incorporated on 7th January 1975 under the Royal Charter of His Majesty the Fourth Druk Gyalpo Jigme Singye Wangchuck. RICB is the only multi-faceted financial service provider in the kingdom catering Life Insurance, General Insurance, Credit Facilities and other Social Security Schemes.

Vision:

To be the premier financial service provider in the country and beyond, seuring the ever evolving aspirations of the society.
